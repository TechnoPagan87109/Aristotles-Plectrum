Private-AI

Members, by themselves, may not always have the time, expertise, or perspective required to identify the highest-satisfaction solutions to complex problems.

To assist with this process, each Member has access to a [Private AI](private-ai.md).

The Private AI acts as an advisor to the Member. It can:

* explain proposals,
* identify trade-offs,
* suggest revisions,
* highlight hidden assumptions,
* and help the Member understand how a proposal aligns with their own goals and priorities.

The Private AI does not vote, make decisions, or represent the Member. Its purpose is to help the Member participate more effectively in the deliberative process.
