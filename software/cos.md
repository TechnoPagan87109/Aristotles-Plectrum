# Community Operating System (COS)

Status 🟢 Draft Complete v1

## Purpose

This document describes the major sections of the digital infrastructure that enables the Community to coordinate governance, resource management, communication, and daily operations.

## Why It Matters

* Enables coordinated action at scale
* Provides visibility into shared systems
* Supports governance and decision-making
* Reduces friction in daily operations

## In This Project

The COS is not a single application. It is a collection of interconnected services that assist Members in operating the Community, coordinating resources, accessing information, and participating in governance.

The COS provides access to the following services:

## Personal Services

* **Private AI**

  * Your guide to the COS and Community.
  * Can assist with any services listed here.

* **Proposal Assistance**

  * Helps Members create, organize, and improve proposals before submission.

* **Education**

  * Provides access to educational and reference materials.

* **Communication**

  * Supports communication between Members and Community systems.

## Governance Services

* **Proposal Creation**

  * Create and submit proposals to the Community.

* **Proposal Review**

  * Review proposals under consideration.

* **Proposal Markup**

  * Comment on, annotate, and suggest improvements to proposals.

* **AI-Assisted Analysis**

  * Helps identify patterns, hidden assumptions, areas of agreement, and sources of disagreement within large amounts of information.

* **Resource Accounting**

  * Provides visibility into Community resources and allocations.
  * See: [Commons Allocation Index](../community/cai.md)

## Infrastructure Services

* **Energy**

  * Monitoring and management of Community energy systems.

* **Water**

  * Monitoring and management of Community water systems.

* **Waste**

  * Monitoring and management of Community waste remediation systems.

* **Monitoring**

  * Visibility into Community infrastructure and operational systems.

## Production Services

* **Workshop Scheduling**

  * Reserve time on shared production equipment.

* **Maintenance Tracking**

  * Track maintenance schedules, service history, and replacement planning.

* **Resource Allocation**

  * Coordinate access to shared production resources.

## Community Services

* **Social Media**

  * Member-only communication and discussion.

* **Entertainment**

  * Access to Community-supported entertainment resources.

* **Training**

  * Access to training materials, guides, and Community knowledge.

## Open Challenge: Security

Security is one of the most important and least-developed areas of the Community Operating System.

The Community requires a security model that is:

* verifiably Open Source,
* accessible only to Members where appropriate,
* capable of protecting Community infrastructure,
* capable of protecting Member privacy,
* resilient against both external and internal threats,
* maintainable by the Community itself.

This project does not yet have a complete answer.

Contributors interested in security, cryptography, authentication, authorization, distributed systems, or Open Source governance are encouraged to participate.

Any solution developed for the Community Operating System should be suitable for release as Open Source software.

## Key Principles

* Transparency
* Accessibility for Members
* Integration across systems
* Human-controlled decision-making
