## Purpose

This document explains the reason behind the name.

Many of Aristotle's positions on issues were forward looking. His position on slavery, not so much. He believed that some people were "natural slaves", without the capacity to look after themselves. He believed that some people have to be slaves because there was that kind of work that needed to be done. His positions were those of someone in the ruling class rationalizing slavery. He did see one possible exception. He saw that "tools that do their own work", by word of command of intelligent understanding, would change the dynamic between people. This community is not about getting free stuff or not having to work at a job (well, kinda is). What this means, at least in our little corner of the world, we would no longer need to treat each other as Rulers and subjects, Overlords and underlings, Masters and slaves. We could just treat each other as equals.
